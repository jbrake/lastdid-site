Long-press any task and tap Insights to open a detailed view of that task's activity over time.

The calendars

You'll see month and year views as color-coded calendars. Each cell is a day; the shade depends on how many times you logged that activity that day. Tap a day to see what you did, and use the arrows to browse between days.

The stats

#

Total — how many times you've ever logged this

📅

This Month / This Year — total for the period you're currently viewing

📆

Active Days — distinct days you've logged it

↔

Avg Interval — typical gap between logs

📅

This Week — count for the current week

📈

vs Last Week — quietly compares this week to last

🔥

Streak — informational, not something to defend

🐢

Longest Gap — the biggest break you've taken

⭐

Busiest Day — the day of the week you tend to do this

For tasks you do multiple times a day, you'll also see a daily average.

Comparing tasks

From inside a heatmap you can pull in up to 3 other tasks to compare side-by-side. Useful for spotting how different activities relate to each other.
