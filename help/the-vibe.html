LastDid is designed to help you notice things, not nag you about them. The whole app is shaped by this idea, and it's worth knowing the choices we made on purpose.

Things LastDid deliberately doesn't do

🔕

No reminders or notifications

🔥

No streaks to protect

⚠

No red warnings or alarm colors

🏆

No achievements, badges, or celebrations

📈

No "you're falling behind" framing

Even the language matters

Wherever the app might use guilt-inducing language, we use something neutral instead.

Overdue → Been a while
X days late → Last logged X days ago
You should... → You usually...
Failed streak → Just the number
Task incomplete → Never logged

The goal is a quiet record of what you've been up to. You know your life best — the app just helps you keep track.
