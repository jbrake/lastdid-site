--- layout: page title: "Color freshness" description: "Why your task colors gently fade over time in LastDid." ---

Task tile colors gently fade the longer it's been since you logged that activity. It's a subtle visual cue — not a warning.

The four levels

Vibrant — Last week or sooner
Slightly faded — 1-2 weeks ago
More faded — 2-4 weeks ago
Quite faded — Over a month ago

Why a fade and not a warning

The fade is desaturation, not a color change. There's no red, no alarm icon, no "you've fallen behind" framing. The point is to help you notice things you used to do — not to make you feel bad about not doing them.

A couple of edge cases

Tasks with a custom interval set keep their icon at full color — the tile's gentle glow handles the visual cue instead, so you're not getting two signals at once.

Tasks you've never logged also show as gently faded, so they're easy to spot in your list.
