--- layout: page title: "Privacy & your data" description: "Where your LastDid data lives and what we don't collect." ---

LastDid stores all your data locally on your device. There are no accounts, no logins, no servers, no sync.

What never leaves your device

🛡

Your task list and folder names

🛡

Every activity log and the notes you've written

🛡

Your custom icons and photos

Nothing in that list is ever sent off your device unless you explicitly export it (and even then, only to where you choose to send it).

What we don't do

No analytics. No telemetry. No tracking. No crash reports that include your activity data. No ads. No "anonymous usage stats."

iCloud device backups

If you have iCloud Backup enabled in iOS Settings, your LastDid data is included in those backups, just like the data of every other app. That's the only path your data takes off your device unless you choose otherwise.

Full details are in the Privacy Policy.
