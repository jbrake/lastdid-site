LastDid is a simple activity journal. It's not a to-do list, and it won't tell you what to do. You log things after you do them, and the app helps you spot patterns over time.

Three ideas at the core

✓

Log after the fact, not before

☰

Notice patterns in your own life

✋

No pressure, no nagging, no guilt

That's the whole app. No reminders, no notifications, no scores, no streaks to protect, no red warnings.
