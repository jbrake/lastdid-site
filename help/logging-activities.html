--- layout: page title: "Logging activities" description: "How to record what you've done in LastDid. One tap to log, with options for notes and undo." ---

Tap any task tile in the Tasks tab to log that you just did it. One tap, that's it.

After logging

A small bar appears at the bottom with two options: Add Note to journal a quick thought about that activity, or Undo if you tapped by mistake. The bar fades away after a few seconds on its own.

Always prompting for a note

If you'd rather always be asked for a note when you log something, turn on "Prompt for note after logging" in Settings.

For specific tasks where you always want to journal — therapy, gratitude, anything reflective — open the task → Edit → Notes → toggle "Always ask for a note". This overrides the global setting just for that one task.
