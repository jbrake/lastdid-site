--- layout: help title: "Backup & restore" summary: "Export and import all your data" description: "Export and import all your LastDid data as a portable JSON file." ---

Settings → Backup & Restore lets you export your full LastDid data as a plain JSON file you can save anywhere — iCloud Drive, Files, AirDrop, the clipboard, your email.

What's included

  • All your tasks (name, color, icon, custom intervals, settings)
  • All your folders
  • Every activity log, including notes
  • Custom photo icons (encoded inline)

Importing

Import from a file or paste from the clipboard. Importing replaces your current dataset, so if you want to keep both your current data and the imported data, export your current state first.

Cross-platform

LastDid for Android uses the exact same JSON format. You can move data freely between iOS and Android.
