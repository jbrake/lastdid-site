Creating a task

Tap the + button in the top right of the Tasks tab. Give your activity a name, pick a color, and optionally choose an icon. Icons can be the default first letter of the name, an emoji, or a photo from your library.

Editing later

Long-press any task tile and tap Edit to change its name, color, icon, custom interval, or note prompt setting.

Deleting

Long-press a task and tap Delete. This also removes all the activity logs you've recorded for that task. There's no undo, so the app will ask you to confirm.

The free task limit

LastDid is free to use for up to 5 activities — enough for most people. If you'd like to track more, you can unlock unlimited tasks from Settings → Unlock Unlimited Tasks. It's a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no recurring fees.
