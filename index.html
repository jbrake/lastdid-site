Not a todo app

LastDid is an activity journal. You log things after you do them, and the app helps you spot patterns over time. No reminders. No guilt.

One-tap logging

Tap a task tile to record that you did it. Add an optional note, or don't. That's the whole interaction.

Colors that fade

Task tiles gently desaturate the longer it's been. A quiet visual cue — no red warnings, no alerts.

Heatmaps & stats

See your activity on a calendar. Streaks, gaps, averages, busiest days — just the facts, presented without judgment.

Completely private

All data stays on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no cloud sync. You own your data.

The best outcome is a user who glances at their phone, thinks "Oh, it's been a while since I called Mom," makes that call, and feels good about it — not because the app told them to, but because the app helped them notice.
