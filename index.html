Not a todo app

LastDid tracks what you've already done. It helps you notice patterns in your life without judgment, guilt, or pressure.

🕐

One-Tap Logging

Tap once to record an activity. That's it. No forms, no categories, no friction.

🌱

Gentle Awareness

"Been a while" — not "overdue." Soft cues help you notice, never nag.

📊

Your Patterns

"You usually do this every ~5 days." Observations based on your behavior, not arbitrary rules.

🔒

Private by Default

All data stays on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no cloud. You own your data completely.

The best outcome is a user who glances at their phone, thinks "Oh, it's been a while since I called Mom," makes that call, and feels good about it — not because the app told them to, but because the app helped them notice.
