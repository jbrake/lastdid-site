LastDid ("the App") is developed by Brake Networks. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Storage

All data you create in LastDid — including tasks, activity logs, and preferences — is stored locally on your device. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

No Data Collection

LastDid does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No third-party SDKs or advertising frameworks
  • No user accounts or registration
  • No cookies or web tracking
  • No location data
  • No access to contacts, photos, camera, or microphone

Device Backups

Your LastDid data is included in standard iPhone and iCloud device backups when you have iCloud Backup enabled. These backups are managed by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Export Feature

LastDid includes an optional export feature that lets you save your data as a JSON file. This export is initiated entirely by you and the data goes only where you choose to save or share it.

In-App Purchases

LastDid offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited tasks. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Brake Networks does not receive or store any payment information.

Core Spotlight

LastDid indexes your task names in iOS Spotlight search to help you find your activities quickly. This data stays on your device and is managed by the operating system.

Children's Privacy

LastDid does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App does not collect information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@brakenetworks.com.
